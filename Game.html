Gaming

This study is all about games.

Bored? "Gamification" increases task engagement

When people are bored, they come up with all kinds of ways to change that... including not following your research study instructions.

Goal: Increase participant engagement in research tasks.
Methods: A/B tests, Survey, Interview
Findings: Participants preferred the task version that included sounds & points. Gamification increased task engagement by 15%.

Participant Feedback: "This is the most fun I've had in a study!"
